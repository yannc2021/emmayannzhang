About Me
AI Researcher

I am an AI researcher with expertise in both hardware and software development. My research focuses on connecting human emotions with technology through multisensory communication and emotionally intelligent AI systems. I am to integrate our sense of touch into AI agents, social robots, and wearable devices, allowing them to sense, interpret and respond to human touch interaction.
